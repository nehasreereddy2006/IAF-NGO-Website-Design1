📌 Why this feature matters The Hero Banner is the most important section of any NGO website. A clear headline stating the mission, a short description, and a strong Call-to-Action button (Donate / Volunteer) can increase donor conversions by up to 40%. The navigation bar should always include a visible "Donate Now" button in a contrasting color.
